# FAQ Content

Selected questions and answers for the website FAQ section.

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| What is Neomind? | Neomind is a mindset app that helps you reshape the way you think and talk to yourself. It blends personalized affirmations, guided visualization, and AI into a short daily practice anyone can follow. |
| Who is Neomind for? | Neomind is for anyone who wants more calm, confidence, or focus in daily life. Whether you're brand new to mindset work or have practiced for years, it meets you where you are. |
| How does the AI personalize my affirmations? | You describe a goal or something you're struggling with, and the AI turns it into affirmations written for your exact situation. The more you use it, the better it understands what resonates with you. |
| Can I use my own voice for affirmations? | Yes. Recording affirmations in your own voice makes them far more powerful, and Neomind lets you do it in seconds, so the voice guiding your mindset is the one you trust most. |
| How much time and effort does this really take? | About five to ten minutes a day. Neomind is built to fit real life, so you can practice during your morning coffee or before bed without adding one more big thing to your list. |
| How soon will I notice a difference? | Many people feel calmer and more focused within the first week. Lasting mindset shifts come with consistency, and Neomind's reminders and streaks help you stick with it. |
| Is my data private and secure? | Yes. Your goals, reflections, and recordings are yours alone. We encrypt your data and never sell it, and your inner work stays private. |
| Is there a free trial? | Yes. You can start a 3 day free trial to test everything Neomind has to offer, with full access to all features. There's also a free plan with a few basic features, so you can keep using the app at no cost. |
| How do I cancel my subscription? | You can cancel anytime directly from your App Store or Google Play account settings, with no emails and no friction. You'll keep premium access until the end of your billing period. |
